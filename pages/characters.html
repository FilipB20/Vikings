CHARACTERS

Ragnar Lodbrok

A legendary Norse hero and warrior, Ragnar was a farmer who became a king through his raids and exploration. He sought to conquer new lands and challenge the gods. His journey is both one of ambition and self-doubt, and his leadership inspired many of the Viking tribes.

Lagertha

A fierce shieldmaiden and queen, Lagertha is one of the most beloved characters in Viking history. Initially Ragnar's wife, Lagertha is a skilled warrior who plays a central role in Ragnar's early conquests. Her strength and leadership skills help shape the Viking world in ways she may not have anticipated.

Bjorn Ironside

As Ragnar’s eldest son, Bjorn was destined for greatness. Growing up amidst battles and exploration, Bjorn develops a strong sense of honor and independence. Throughout the series, he embarks on journeys of his own, seeking to build his legacy while grappling with the complexity of family dynamics.

Ivar the Boneless

Born with a disability, Ivar becomes one of the most ruthless and brilliant Viking leaders. Despite his physical condition, Ivar's cunning mind and thirst for power lead him to victories and betrayals that alter the fate of his family and his people. His complex relationship with his brothers often spirals into violence.

Rollo

Ragnar's brother, Rollo is a character of great inner conflict. Initially loyal to Ragnar, Rollo's ambition and desire for power drive him to make a shocking decision—betraying his own family to forge a new path as a ruler in France. His journey highlights the theme of loyalty, betrayal, and personal ambition.

Floki

A close friend of Ragnar and a devout believer in the Norse gods, Floki is a master shipbuilder and an eccentric character. His unwavering loyalty to Ragnar and his deeply spiritual connection to the gods shape his role in the Viking world. However, his journey is fraught with emotional turmoil, as he grapples with his faith and friendships.

Ubbe

The eldest son of Ragnar and Lagertha, Ubbe is known for his wisdom and leadership. He is the calm and sensible counterpart to his more unpredictable brothers. Throughout the series, Ubbe’s character evolves as he seeks to honor his father's legacy while facing the harsh realities of Viking life and warfare.

Torvi

Torvi is a warrior who demonstrates immense loyalty, strength, and determination. She becomes a prominent character as she marries Bjorn, and her role evolves as she helps him navigate both personal and political struggles. Torvi’s loyalty to Bjorn is tested time and again as the Viking world around them shifts.

Aslaug

Aslaug is Ragnar’s second wife and a woman of great strength and intelligence. Her background as a daughter of a famous warrior plays a key role in her interaction with Ragnar. Her complex personality—marked by both wisdom and jealousy—creates tension in her relationship with Ragnar and his first wife, Lagertha.

Character Relationships

Ragnar and Lagertha

The relationship between Ragnar and Lagertha is one of deep love, respect, and conflict. Their partnership begins with mutual admiration and grows through their shared challenges. Despite their separation, they continue to impact each other's lives profoundly.

Bjorn and Ivar

As brothers, Bjorn and Ivar share a complex and often antagonistic relationship. Their differing values—Bjorn’s desire for peace and Ivar’s thirst for power—create tension and conflict, particularly as their paths diverge in the later seasons.

Rollo and Ragnar

The bond between Ragnar and Rollo is one of both brotherhood and betrayal. Rollo's eventual decision to align with the Franks creates a deep rift between him and Ragnar, despite their early years as allies.

Floki and Ragnar

Floki's bond with Ragnar is one of trust, loyalty, and mutual understanding, but it is also tested by betrayal and differing views on faith. Floki’s actions deeply affect Ragnar, particularly in the later stages of their friendship.

Ragnar and Aslaug

The relationship between Ragnar and Aslaug was one of power, faith, and tension. While Ragnar respects Aslaug’s abilities, their marriage faced many obstacles, including Aslaug’s jealousy and her conflict with Lagertha.

Bjorn and Torvi

Bjorn and Torvi’s relationship is based on mutual respect and support. Torvi stood by Bjorn through battles, and they later had children together. Their relationship grows stronger with time, showing the importance of loyalty and love in the Viking world.

Ubbe and Ivar

The relationship between Ubbe and Ivar is one of rivalry and resentment. While Ubbe values peace and leadership, Ivar is consumed by vengeance. This division creates ongoing tension throughout their journey as brothers.
