Vikings is a historical drama television series inspired by the sagas of Ragnar Lodbrok, a legendary Norse hero. The series follows his journey as a farmer who rises to become a Scandinavian king through raids, battles, and alliances. The show delves into Viking culture, mythology, and historical events with dramatic storytelling.
